Welcome to my blog! This is where I write about my ideas, latest projects, and things that I recently learned about. After learning so many things through other people's blogs, I finally decided to create my own, as a way to give back to the tech community that I've relied so heavily on.

If you find any issues, you can open a new issue on this blog's repository: github.com/Jeansidharta/blog or email me at jeansidharta@gmail.com

The basic idea of this blog is to share some of my ideas and some of the things I've been recently learning about. The format of a post is to write from my own point of view, as some sort of narrator, and to have audience members, represented by animals, interacting and responding to the text. The animals are intended to be caricatures of people; stereotypes of different possible audience members. They're my predictions of what the audience migh be currently thinking, and gives me an opportuinity to respond.
